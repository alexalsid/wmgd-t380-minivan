INTERIORS

The comfortable interior and technical features of our vans bring you to your destinations relaxed and in style. Your space can be transformed into a mobile office, relaxing lounge, or personal dining area in a matter of moments.

INSIDE THE
MARCO POLO

The Marco Polo is perfect for relaxing, holding conference meetings, and even dining in style. This van model seats up to six passengers including the driver. The front includes two comfort seats with a steering wheel and gear shift lever in Nappa leather. The luxury comfort style continues to the back with two to four rear seats with a variable rail system, and a fold out table with bottle and cupholders. With swivelling front seats, you can be sure you’ll never miss any of the conversation.

The optional dining interior includes a gas hob, sink, and refrigerator in addition to the fold out table. You’ll be able to enjoy your meals on the go or even have them made for you as you travel.

All interiors are made from luxury materials including customizable Lugano leather seats and high quality yacht wood flooring— and have numerous storage spaces for luggage of any size. Two 12 volt sockets allow you to charge your devices on the go.

INSIDE THE SPRINTER

The Sprinter is perfect for lounging, enjoying a meal and entertaining as you travel. This van model seats a variable amount of passengers. The front includes two comfort seats with a steering wheel and gear shift lever in Nappa leather. With a swivelling passenger seat, you’ll never miss any of the action.

The rear of the Sprinter includes a seating bench, television, kitchenette, restroom, closet and customizable LED lighting. The kitchenette includes a gas hob, sink, and refrigerator. Overhead storage is perfect for kitchen and glassware, allowing you to enjoy a meal or a drink as you travel in style.

The interior is made from quality materials— including customizable Lugano leather seats and high quality yacht wood flooring— and has numerous storage spaces for luggage. Two 12 volt sockets allow you to charge your devices on the go.
